## Our Light and Laser Options

We offer three Sciton light and laser treatments that can be used either on their own or combined for more comprehensive results. 

### BroadBand Light (BBL®) HERO™ 

*The gold standard in pulsed light therapy*

* **How it Works:** [BBL HERO](https://lazaderm.com/services/broadband-light-bbl-hero) delivers high-energy pulses of broad-spectrum light to break down melanin and hemoglobin and trigger new collagen production.  
* **Best for:** Sun damage, age spots, facial redness, broken capillaries and uneven pigmentation. 

### MOXI™ Laser

*A gentle entry into laser skin care*

* **How it Works:** [MOXI](https://lazaderm.com/services/moxi-laser%20) creates microthermal zones in the skin to trigger collagen production and renewal.  
* **Best For:** Melasma, sun spots, fine lines and uneven skin tone.

### HALO® Hybrid Fractional Laser

*A complete skin makeover in one treatment*

* **How it Works:** [HALO](https://lazaderm.com/services/the-halo-laser) is the world’s first hybrid fractional laser, with both ablative and non-ablative wavelengths to resurface the top layer while heating deeper layers.   
* **Best For:** Fine to moderate wrinkles, texture concerns, pigmentation, scarring and enlarged pores.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do laser treatments hurt?

Comfort is our priority at Lazaderm. Lasers can feel warm and sometimes like a quick snap on the skin. Several measures can keep you comfortable during your treatment, including:

* Integrated cooling technology  
* Topical numbing cream  
* Fast and efficient sessions  
* Providers who can adjust energy levels to keep you in your comfort zone 

### How long is the downtime with lasers?

The downtime will depend on the type of device we are using. BBL HERO and MOXI do not remove the surface of the skin and come with minimal to no downtime. HALO comes with a few days of redness and peeling as it refreshes the skin’s surface.

### Can laser treatments be combined?

Yes, we often combine laser treatments in one treatment plan. MOXI and BBL are commonly paired to treat both discoloration and texture concerns in one appointment. We may suggest using each laser at different times to boost the skin’s collagen production and keep the tone even throughout the year.  

### Can lasers be used on dark skin?

Some lasers are suitable for dark skin tones because they target deeper layers rather than the surface of the skin. Other lasers may not be suitable. The best way to find out if a certain treatment is right for your skin is to schedule a [free consultation](https://lazaderm.com/free-consultation) at Lazaderm. We will find treatments that address your concerns while being safe for your skin type.
