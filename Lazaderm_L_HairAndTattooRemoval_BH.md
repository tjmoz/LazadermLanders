## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does laser hair removal hurt? 

Most patients compare it to having a rubber band snapped against the skin. Our lasers have built-in cooling that prevents the surface of the skin from getting too warm. Laser hair removal is more comfortable than waxing. 

### Does laser tattoo removal hurt? 

There is discomfort with this treatment, but we keep each session as short as possible. We use comfort measures to make the process tolerable, and you can use topical lidocaine on smaller tattoos to help. 

### How many sessions will I need? 

Hair removal can provide visible results in five to eight sessions. These will be spaced several weeks apart to match your hair’s growth cycle. Tattoo removal requires 5-15 sessions, depending on the size, color and location of the tattoo. We will give you an estimate during your consultation. 

### Is there downtime after laser hair and tattoo removal? 

Laser hair removal has zero downtime, and you can return to most normal activities right away. Tattoo removal can produce temporary redness and blisters. 

### Will tattoo removal leave a scar? 

Quanta Q-Plus is designed to minimize scarring. It has ultra-short pulse durations to reduce heat damage to the surrounding tissue. It will not resolve any current scars.

### Can you treat dark skin tones? 

Yes, we offer Nd:YAG laser technology as part of our suite of laser devices, which is suitable for a wider range of skin types, including deeper complexions, because it penetrates beneath the skin. However, we require a [free consultation](https://lazaderm.com/free-consultation) for all of our patients to make sure our treatments are the right fit.
