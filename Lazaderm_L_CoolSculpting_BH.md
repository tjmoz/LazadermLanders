## Frequently Asked Questions

### How is CoolSculpting Elite different from the older system? 

The Elite system uses newly designed applicators that have 18% more cooling coverage. It can run two applicators simultaneously, so you can treat multiple areas in a single sitting rather than doing one area at a time. These treatments are also priced by area rather than by cycle, so you end up paying less to treat one area. You get shorter appointments, better contact with the skin and improved results.

### What body areas can you treat with CoolSculpting Elite? 

CoolSculpting Elite is FDA-cleared for nine zones: abdomen (upper and lower), flanks, inner and outer thighs, under the chin, below the jawline, bra fat, back fat, upper arms and the banana roll (under the buttocks). During your consultation, we will create a treatment plan that targets your desired areas in as few treatments as possible.

### What does CoolSculpting Elite feel like? 

You will feel a firm suction and an intense cold for the first five minutes. After this, the area goes numb and most patients read or watch something on their phone. For most people, it feels more unusual than uncomfortable.

### When will I see my CoolSculpting Elite results?

You may see some changes after three weeks. Your body needs time to flush out the treated cells, and it can take several months and multiple sessions to see your full results. 

### Is CoolSculpting Elite permanent? 

The fat cells that are treated do not come back. However, your results can change with weight gain because the remaining fat cells can still expand. Maintaining your weight keeps your results stable.
